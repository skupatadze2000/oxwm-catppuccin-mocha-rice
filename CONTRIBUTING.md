# Contributing to oxwm-catppuccin-mocha-rice

I luv dat u wanna contribute! Whether it's fixing a bug, improving the config, or adding a new theme variant, contributions are welcome totally.

1. **Fork the repo.**
2. **Create a branch** for your change
3. **Commit your changes.**
4. **Push to the branch.**
5. **Open a Pull Request! (ill agree if I see it, ill be active)**
